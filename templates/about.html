About SignLingua

Bridging communication gaps through Indian Sign Language translation

Our Mission

To empower the Deaf and hard‑of‑hearing community by providing real‑time, accurate sign language translation using cutting‑edge AI technology. We aim to make communication seamless and inclusive.

Our Vision

A world where language is no barrier – where sign language users can interact freely with anyone, anywhere, using just a webcam and our intelligent platform.

Our Values

Inclusivity, innovation, and community-driven development. We work closely with the Deaf community to ensure our translations are culturally accurate and respectful.

How It Works

1. Choose Your Input

Upload a sign language video, type text, or use your webcam to sign in real‑time.

2. AI Translation

Our CNN‑Transformer model processes your input and recognises the sign.

3. Output

Get text, speech, and a 3D avatar animation in your chosen language (English, Hindi, Telugu).
